About me:

I'm a final year PhD student at the center for cosmology and particle physics (CCPP) at New York University (NYU), working on the Large Scale Structure of the universe with Roman Scoccimarro. I also work on dark matter phenomenology using astrophysical systems. Recently, my interest has been piqued by neural networks and I've used them to emulate expensive cosmological hydrodyanic simulations. Before moving to NYC, I was an undergraduate student in Engineering Physics at IIT Bombay in India.
