I'm a final year PhD student at the Center for cosmology and Particle Physics (CCPP) at New York University (NYU). I work on the Large Scale Structure of the universe with Prof. Roman Scoccimarro and on innteracting Dark Matter models with Prof. Glennys Farrar. I also work on using neural networks to emulate cosmological hydrodyanic simulations. Before moving to NYC, I was an undergraduate student in Engineering Physics at IIT Bombay in India.
